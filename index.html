Shopping List:







Instructions:

To Add Item:

Enter item name into the field and then press the "Add Item" button

To Move Item:

Enter the number of the item you want to move. Press "Enter".

Then keep pressing the arrow buttons to keep moving the item you selected.

To Change the Item Selected:

Just enter the new item number into the forma nd press the "Enter" button.

To Remove Item:

Enter the number of item you want to remove into the "Remove Item #:".

Then press the "Remove Item" button.
